A brief History of the Shop

Adom Ne Asomdwoe Hardware Shop was established in 2015 with a simple vision: to provide quality building materials at affordable prices to the people of Kumasi and beyond. What started as a small shop in Agogo Kopas has grown into a trusted name in the construction industry, serving homeowners, contractors, and builders across Ghana. Over the years, we have built strong relationships with top manufacturers like Ghacem, Dangote, and Supacem, ensuring that every product on our shelves meets the highest standards. Our commitment to reliability and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to hardware shop for both small renovations and large-scale construction projects.

What Our Shop Is All About

At Adom Ne Asomdwoe Hardware Shop, we believe that every structure begins with quality materials. Whether you're building a family home, a commercial property, or simply renovating, we are here to supply everything you need. We take pride in being part of your building journey, from the foundation to the finishing touches.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality building materials at competitive prices while delivering exceptional customer service. We strive to be the most trusted hardware partner for every builder, contractor, and homeowner in Ghana by ensuring product availability, timely delivery, and expert guidance on every purchase.

Our Vision

To become the leading hardware and building materials supplier in Ghana, recognized for excellence in quality, service, and reliability. We envision expanding to serve communities across the country while maintaining the personal touch and trust that our customers have come to expect from us.
