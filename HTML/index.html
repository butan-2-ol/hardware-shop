About Adom Ne Asomdwoe Hardware Shop

Adom Ne Asomdwoe Hardware Shop is a family-owned business dedicated to serving the building and construction needs of our community. Located in Agogo Kopas, Kumasi, we offer a wide range of products including cement, iron rods, nails, paints, plumbing materials, electrical supplies, and tools.

With over 10 years of experience in the hardware industry, our team understands the challenges of construction projects and is committed to helping you find the right materials at the right price. We offer nationwide delivery, bulk purchase discounts, and personalized service to ensure your project runs smoothly. Whether you are a professional contractor or a first-time builder, we welcome you to experience the Adom Ne Asomdwoe difference.
